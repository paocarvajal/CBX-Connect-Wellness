2. The Proposal: "CBX Connect Wellness" - A Phased Approach

Phase 1: Concierge & Partnership (Year 1)

Establish immediate revenue with minimal capital expenditure.

  • Strategic Alliances: Create a network of "CBX Certified" clinics.
  • Tiered Packages: Offer B2B (Connect) and VIP (DTC) services.
  • Revenue: Commission fees & direct VIP package sales.

Phase 2: The Physical Hub (Years 2-3)

Integrate CBX Connect Wellness into the new $100M physical expansion.

  • Medical Recovery Hotel: A dedicated wing for patient recovery.
  • CBX Health Welcome Center: A physical hub for partner clinics.

Phase 3: The Digital Platform (Ongoing)

Leverage technology to own the customer journey from start to finish.

  • CBX App Portal: A dedicated "CBX Connect Wellness" section.
  • Key Features: Clinic directory, booking, document management, and concierge chat.
